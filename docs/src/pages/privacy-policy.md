Yaala Labs is the data controller for personal data processed through this website. For privacy-related enquiries, please contact us via our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/yaalalabs/agent-kernel/issues) or at [privacy@yaalalabs.com](mailto:privacy@yaalalabs.com).

## What Data We Collect and Why

### Analytics Data (with your consent)

We use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to collect anonymized, aggregated statistics about how visitors use this documentation, including which pages are viewed, approximate geographic region (country/city level), browser type, and how users navigate between pages. This helps us improve the documentation.

**Legal basis:** Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR / UK GDPR). Analytics data is collected **only if you explicitly accept cookies** via the consent banner displayed on your first visit. You may withdraw consent at any time (see [Withdrawing Consent](#withdrawing-consent) below). Declining or withdrawing consent has no effect on your ability to access the site.

We have configured GA4 with:

- **IP anonymization:** Your IP address is truncated by Google before storage; the full IP address is never written to disk and we never receive it
- **No advertising features:** We do not use Google Signals, remarketing, or any ad-targeting functionality
- **No cross-site tracking:** Data is used solely for aggregate analytics on this property

For details on which cookies GA4 sets, see our [Cookie Policy](/cookie-policy).

### Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any other directly identifying personal data. We do not require accounts or registration to use this site.

## Your Rights

If you are located in the EEA, United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with applicable data protection law, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

- **Right of access:** Request a copy of personal data we hold about you
- **Right to rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Right to erasure:** Request deletion of your data
- **Right to restrict processing:** Request that we limit how we use your data
- **Right to data portability:** Request your data in a portable format
- **Right to object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to withdraw consent:** Withdraw analytics consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal
- **Right to lodge a complaint:** You have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority (e.g., the ICO in the UK, or your EEA member state's authority)

Because we process only anonymized, aggregated analytics data (and only with your consent), we are unlikely to hold data that is identifiable to you individually. However, we will respond to any rights request in good faith.

## Withdrawing Consent

You may withdraw your analytics consent at any time by:

1. Clearing the `ak_cookie_consent` entry from your browser's local storage for this site
2. Deleting the `_ga` and `_ga_TFXXDHX2H5` cookies via your browser tools
3. Installing [Google's Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

See our [Cookie Policy](/cookie-policy) for step-by-step instructions.
