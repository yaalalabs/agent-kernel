## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

## Our Use of Cookies

**This website does not use cookies.**

Specifically:
- We do not use cookies to track your browsing behavior
- We do not use cookies to store personal information
- We do not use cookies for analytics or advertising purposes
- We do not use session cookies or persistent cookies
- We do not use first-party or third-party cookies

## Similar Technologies

In addition to not using cookies, we also do not use:
