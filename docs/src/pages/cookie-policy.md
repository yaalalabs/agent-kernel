# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated: June 17, 2026**

## Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Yaala Labs ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies on the Agent Kernel documentation website at [kernel.yaala.ai](https://kernel.yaala.ai), and how you can control them.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site operators about how visitors interact with their content.

## Cookie Categories We Use

### Analytics Cookies (non-essential, consent required)

We use **Google Analytics 4 (GA4)** to collect anonymized, aggregated data about how visitors use this documentation. This helps us understand which pages are most useful and improve the content over time.

| Cookie | Provider | Purpose | Duration |
|--------|----------|---------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google LLC | Distinguishes unique visitors by assigning a randomly generated identifier | 2 years |
| `_ga_TFXXDHX2H5` | Google LLC | Stores and counts page views for this GA4 property | 2 years |

**Legal basis:** Consent (Article 6(1)(a) of the UK/EU General Data Protection Regulation). These cookies are only placed after you explicitly click **Accept Analytics** on the consent banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and no data is sent to Google.

### Essential Storage (no consent required)

This website does not set any essential cookies. The Docusaurus framework stores your search preferences and (if applicable) color mode preference in your browser's `localStorage` or `sessionStorage`. These entries never leave your device and are not cookies.

Your cookie consent choice is stored in `localStorage` under the key `ak_cookie_consent` so you are not shown the banner on return visits.

## IP Anonymization

We have enabled IP anonymization in our GA4 configuration. Your IP address is truncated by Google before it is stored, and the full IP address is never written to disk. We never receive or process your full IP address.

## How We Obtain Consent

When you first visit this site, a consent banner is displayed at the bottom of the page. Analytics cookies are **not set** until you explicitly click **Accept Analytics**. If you click **Decline**, no analytics cookies are placed and no data is sent to Google Analytics for your visit.

Consent is not a condition of accessing this site. You may browse all documentation without accepting analytics cookies.

## Withdrawing or Changing Your Consent

You may withdraw or change your consent at any time:

1. **Clear local storage:** Open your browser's developer tools, go to Application > Local Storage > `https://kernel.yaala.ai`, and delete the `ak_cookie_consent` entry. The consent banner will reappear on your next visit.
2. **Delete cookies:** Use your browser's cookie management tools to delete `_ga` and `_ga_TFXXDHX2H5` cookies for this domain.
3. **Google opt-out:** Install [Google's Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) to block Google Analytics across all sites you visit.

## Third-Party Data Processing

When you accept analytics cookies, your anonymized usage data is sent to and processed by **Google LLC** (United States) under Google's Data Processing Terms and Standard Contractual Clauses. See [Google's Privacy & Terms](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for full details.

External links on this site (GitHub, PyPI, Terraform Registry, Discord, etc.) may set their own cookies when you navigate to those sites. We have no control over those cookies.
